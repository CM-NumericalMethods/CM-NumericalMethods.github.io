The Masters Quant Lab is intended for investigating a rigorous, first-principles approach to model risk by treating
liquidity and volatility as dynamic fields rather than static variables. The work of this laboratory
exists at the interface between mathematical physics and quantitative finance, seeking to identify
vulnerabilities that standard backtesting often misses. The lab also houses other related projects
of theoretical interest.
